Information about University

Astana IT University was founded to become a leading center of education and science in the field of information technology in Kazakhstan.

Astana IT University is a modern university focused on training specialists in the field of information technology and engineering.

Our students are trained in advanced educational programs that include both theoretical knowledge and practical skills for a successful career in the IT field.

The university actively cooperates with international universities and leading IT companies, providing students with unique opportunities for study and internships.

Fields of Study:

  • Big Data Analysis
  • Smart Technologies
  • Software Engineering
  • IT Management
  • Cybersecurity

What kind of advantage will you take after bachelor's degree

At AITU, students gain not only theoretical knowledge, but also real experience in the IT field, completing internships in large international companies and taking part in startups.
